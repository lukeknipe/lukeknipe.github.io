Hi, I'm Luke.

This is a personal website I created for sharing maps, pieces of writing, photos, etc.

I currently work for the Pima County Communications Office, where I write and edit all sorts of material, create web and social media content, and help the County share information with the news media and general public.

For more about my work background, check out my LinkedIn profile.

I am interested in computer technology, especially geographic information systems (GIS), databases, analytics, visualizations, and infographics. I am especially interested in free, open-source implementations of these things, and in the World Wide Web as a means of sharing them.
