Wifi programmable irrigation controller using SONOFF 4CH R3

November 20, 2023

The SONOFF 4CH R3 is an ideal starting point for a DYI irrigation controller. Like the all the other SONOFF smart switches, the 4CH R3 is built on the Espressif ESP8266 chip, which can be flashed with free and open-source firmware like Tasmota. But what makes the 4CH R3 special is its four dry relays.

A "dry relay" is a relay that switches a circuit separate and isolated from the one that powers the relay. That's important for this application, because our irrigation valves run on 24 volts AC.

Most of the SONOFF smart switch products are "wet" designs that are powered by 100 - 240V household voltage, and output that same voltage from their relays when switched on. That works great if you're using it to power something that takes household current, like a lamp. But it doesn't work when you need 24 volts AC.
